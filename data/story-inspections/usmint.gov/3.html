Gifts for Dads

Celebrate the fathers in your life with wonderful gifts from the United States Mint’s collection of unique gifts for dads. Look for pieces to enhance his own collection or discover the most recent releases. You’ll also find timeless pieces capturing iconic American emblems such as Lady Liberty, the American eagle, and the Declaration of Independence. Whether your gift will begin a new tradition or continue a long-standing one, you are sure to find a treasure to make his Father’s Day that much brighter.
