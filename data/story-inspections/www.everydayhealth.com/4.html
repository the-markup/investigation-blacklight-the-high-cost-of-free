You’ve Been Diagnosed With Metastatic NSCLC: What’s Next?

Non-small-cell lung cancer (NSCLC) is the most common type of lung cancer, accounting for 80 to 85 percent of all cases. When the cancer has spread from your lungs to other parts of your body, it’s referred to as metastatic.
