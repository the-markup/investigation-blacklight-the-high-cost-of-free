Open Trans Service - Facing the Fights Ahead

Kara Zajac Oct 21, 2017 0 Comments

21 October 2017

Our director Blake along with Karen Kendra Holmes (Maryland Self-Defense Forces), Paula Neira (USNA, 1985), Bree Fram (USAF), and Fiona Dawson (TransMilitary) participated in OutServe-SLDN's 6th annual LGBT Military Leadership Conference panel titled, "Open Trans Service - Facing the Fights Ahead." This panel was moderated by Chris Johnson (Washington Blade) and addressed the difficulities that trans service members are facing when they come out at work.

Disclaimer: The views and opinions expressed in this video are those of the panelists and do not necessarily reflect the official policy or position of any agency of the U.S. government.

SPART*A

SPART*A is a group of transgender people who currently serve or have served in the military. Membership is open to transgender (to include non-binary and gender-nonconforming) individuals currently serving in the United States Armed Forces. We also offer smaller groups for families of transgender service members, veterans, and allies.
